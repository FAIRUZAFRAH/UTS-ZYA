teaching children to
recite the Al Qu'ran

Teaching young children to recite the Quran is an activity filled with patience and meaning. It not only involves introducing the Arabic letters and Quranic recitation but also instills spiritual and moral values from an early age. By using fun approaches, such as visual aids, stories, and games, children can more easily understand and become interested in learning. Additionally, the teacher needs to build a warm relationship so that the children feel comfortable and enthusiastic about their lessons. Patience and consistency are crucial in helping them gradually develop their Quranic recitation skills. Photo by José Alejandro Cuffia on Unsplash
